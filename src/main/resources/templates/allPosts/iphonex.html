Experience the sophistication and power of the iPhone X, crafted to captivate with its cutting-edge features and stylish design.

Launched in 2017, the iPhone X quickly gained popularity among Nigerian tech enthusiasts who appreciate advanced technology paired with refined aesthetics. As a groundbreaking model, the iPhone X introduced several innovations that set new standards in smartphones:

  • Expansive OLED Screen: A high-contrast, edge-to-edge display perfect for viewing photos, watching videos, and immersive browsing.
  • Facial Recognition (Face ID): A convenient and secure method of unlocking, eliminating the home button for a more intuitive experience.
  • Powerful A11 Bionic Processor: Provides smooth operation and power efficiency, supporting multitasking, gaming, and app use seamlessly.
  • Dual-Camera System: Optimized for Portrait Mode and low-light conditions, delivering exceptional photo quality for diverse settings.

Today, the iPhone X remains a top choice in Nigeria, balancing form and functionality to satisfy both tech-savvy individuals and lifestyle-oriented users.

iPhone X Battery Capacity

The iPhone X delivers notable battery life, running up to 2 hours longer than the iPhone 7. It supports extensive talk time, with up to 21 hours on a single charge for regular users.

For internet browsing, the iPhone X lasts up to 12 hours, while video playback reaches 13 hours for uninterrupted viewing. For audio playback, users can enjoy music and podcasts for an impressive 60 hours.

Additionally, the iPhone X supports fast charging, allowing 50% charge in just 30 minutes when using a compatible adapter. It includes a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery and is compatible with Qi-certified wireless chargers, providing flexibility. Alternatively, users can charge the device via USB using a computer or power adapter.

iPhone X Connectivity Options

The iPhone X provides an extensive selection of connectivity technologies, ensuring seamless connections whether you're on the go or at home.

Cellular and Wireless Capabilities

The iPhone X supports a variety of wireless technologies to ensure robust cellular and internet connections:

  • Model A1865 supports multiple cellular technologies, including:
    • FDD-LTE on multiple bands, such as 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 12, 13, 17, 18, 19, 20, 25, 26, 28, 29, 30, and 66.
    • TD-LTE on bands 34, 38, 39, 40, and 41.
    • Additional support for TD-SCDMA, CDMA EV-DO, UMTS, and GSM/EDGE networks.
  • Model A1901 lacks CDMA network support but retains compatibility with most global bands and wireless technologies.

Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Features

For seamless wireless connectivity, the iPhone X offers:

  • 802.11ac Wi-Fi with MIMO technology for faster wireless speeds.
  • Bluetooth 5.0 for improved range and stability in wireless connections.
  • NFC with reader mode for easy payments and data sharing.

Location Services

The iPhone X ensures precise location tracking with several technologies, including:

  • Assisted GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and QZSS for accurate positioning.
  • A built-in digital compass for enhanced navigation.
  • Wi-Fi and cellular triangulation for improved location services.
  • iBeacon microlocation for accurate indoor positioning.

Audio and Video Calling

The iPhone X excels in calling functionality with:

  • FaceTime for high-quality video calls over Wi-Fi or cellular.
  • FaceTime Audio for clear voice calls.
  • Support for Voice over LTE (VoLTE) and Wi-Fi calling for enhanced call quality.

Audio Playback Support

The iPhone X offers a wide range of audio format compatibility, including:

  • AAC-LC, HE-AAC, HE-AAC v2, Protected AAC, MP3, and Linear PCM.
  • Apple Lossless, FLAC, and various Dolby Digital formats.
  • Audible formats such as 2, 3, 4, Enhanced Audio, AAX, and AAX+.

Video Playback Capabilities

The iPhone X supports a wide array of video formats, such as:

  • HEVC, H.264, MPEG-4 Part 2, and Motion JPEG formats.
  • High Dynamic Range (HDR) content with Dolby Vision and HDR10 support.
  • Video mirroring and output up to 1080p via Lightning Digital AV Adapter and Lightning to VGA Adapter.

Siri Capabilities

Siri on the iPhone X empowers users to perform tasks hands-free, including:

  • Sending messages, setting reminders, and managing tasks through voice commands.
  • Receiving intelligent app suggestions for apps like Messages and Mail.
  • Activating Siri with the “Hey Siri” voice command for hands-free use.
  • Identifying songs and providing other audio-related services.

iPhone X Operating System

The iPhone X runs on iOS 11, a cutting-edge operating system that introduces advanced features to elevate both productivity and user experience. iOS 11 makes the iPhone more powerful, intuitive, and personalized, delivering a seamless, intelligent interface.

iPhone X Headphones

The iPhone X is bundled with EarPods with Lightning Connector, offering a superior audio experience. These wired earbuds are ergonomically designed to fit snugly in your ears while delivering rich, clear sound for an immersive listening experience.

iPhone X SIM Card

The iPhone X uses a Nano-SIM, which means it is incompatible with the larger micro-SIM cards that some users may have. Ensure you’re using the correct SIM card to fully unlock the device’s potential and ensure smooth functionality.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) About iPhone X:

How much is iPhone X in Nigeria?

The price of the iPhone X in Nigeria varies based on the retailer and the condition of the device (new or used). Generally, it can range from approximately ₦150,000 to ₦250,000.

Is iPhone X expensive?

Considering its features and capabilities, the iPhone X is viewed as a premium device and can be considered expensive compared to other smartphones, especially when it was first released.

Is the iPhone X camera good?

Yes, the iPhone X features a dual-camera system with a 12MP wide-angle and telephoto lens. It offers excellent photo quality, including impressive low-light performance, Portrait mode, and Optical Image Stabilization (OIS) for both photos and videos.

Is the iPhone X waterproof?

The iPhone X is rated IP67 for water and dust resistance, meaning it can withstand submersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes, making it resistant to splashes, spills, and brief submersion.

Does the iPhone X have fingerprint?

No, the iPhone X does not have a fingerprint sensor; instead, it uses Face ID for facial recognition and unlocking the device.

Is the iPhone X wireless charging?

Yes, the iPhone X supports wireless charging using Qi-certified chargers, allowing for convenient charging without cables.

What is the RAM in iPhone X?

The iPhone X comes with 3GB of RAM, which supports smooth multitasking and app performance.

Is the iPhone X 5G?

No, the iPhone X does not support 5G connectivity as it was released before 5G technology became mainstream. It supports 4G LTE for fast data speeds.

What was the iPhone X launch price?

The iPhone X was launched at a price of $999 in the United States, which may vary based on the region and local taxes. Prices for new models could fluctuate over time due to availability and condition.

Can iPhone X use 5G WIFI?

Yes, the iPhone X can connect to Wi-Fi networks, including 5GHz networks, but it does not support 5G cellular networks. It can utilize faster Wi-Fi speeds where available.

How much is iPhone X battery in Nigeria?

The price of an iPhone X battery replacement in Nigeria can range from ₦30,000 to ₦50,000, depending on the service provider and the quality of the replacement battery.

iPhone X battery price in Nigeria?

The battery itself typically costs around ₦15,000 to ₦30,000 if purchased separately, though prices may vary based on brand and source.
